Vehicle Repair Module

Purpose:
- Repairs damaged cars using a mechanic, spare parts, and cash from inventory.

Business Rules:
- Car must exist and be in damaged status.
- Mechanic must be registered and must have mechanic role assigned.
- Spare part must exist with enough quantity.
- Cash balance must be enough to cover repair cost.

Effects after successful repair:
- Car status changes from damaged to available.
- Spare part quantity is reduced (and removed if quantity reaches 0).
- Cash balance is reduced by repair cost.
- Repair record is saved in vehicle repair history.

TUI Access:
- Main Menu -> 7) Vehicle Repair Module
- 1) Repair damaged vehicle
- 2) List repair records

Inputs required for repair:
- Damaged car name
- Mechanic name
- Spare part name
- Spare part quantity
- Repair cost


Reputation Module

Purpose:
- Tracks each crew member's street reputation points and keeps a history log of changes.

Business Rules:
- Crew member must be registered before reputation can be changed.
- Point change cannot be zero.
- Reason is required for every reputation change.

Features:
- Add reputation points.
- Deduct reputation points.
- View reputation standings (sorted by highest points).
- View reputation logs (all members or filtered by member name).

TUI Access:
- Main Menu -> 8) Reputation Module
- 1) Add reputation points
- 2) Deduct reputation points
- 3) List reputation standings
- 4) List reputation logs

Inputs for reputation update:
- Crew member name
- Points value
- Reason
